About Me


Welcome to the 'about me' section of my website. Let's jump right in! I've been coding since 2016, working in both JavaScript and Ruby. In that time, I've also learned a handful of useful full-stack technologies, including Node.js, Express, Angular.js, Rails, and SaSS, to name a few. A comprehensive list can be found below.

I'm very passionate about developing software - it gives me an outlet for my creativity while allowing me to pursue my career in software engineering. I enjoy the balance of design and technology inherent in web development. Hopefully, as you explore this website, my passion shines through :)

When I'm not building software, I'm crazy about the outdoors. I grew up in southeastern Tennessee and absolutely adore the mountains. I thoroughly enjoy hiking, camping, climbing, and backpacking. Years ago, I worked as an organic gardener. Today, I still enjoy tending plants, especially succulents. My dog, Indigo, is a beagle mix and my most loyal companion. I also enjoy pinball and all kinds of gaming. Other hobbies include game development, computer security, philosophy, and history.

My favorite food is pepperoni pizza.
